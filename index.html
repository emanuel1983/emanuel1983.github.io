About me

Me, myself and I

Hey people! I'm Emanuel, a multimedia professional currently working as a New Media Manager in London and much of my work is based on using different content management systems as well as updating pages via text editors. I also look after interactives across the UK and have recently starting creating my own.

I have over 8 years experience creating digital products for the web and have recently redesigned the whole National Portrait Gallery website myself without spending a penny. That's a lot of css work right there. I also specialise in UX and pride myself in making websites that are beautiful but above all usable for every type of audience.
